About Us

A Legacy of Devotion and Heritage

Established in 1835 and originally known as the Sunnambu Kambam, Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple stands as a testament to faith and cultural heritage in Little India, Singapore. Generations of devotees have found solace and divine blessings within its walls.

Rebuilt with a majestic Rajagopuram in 1987 and further enhanced over the years, the temple continues to be a vibrant center for spiritual and cultural gatherings.
